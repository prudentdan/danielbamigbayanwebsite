About Daniel Bamigbayan

Daniel Bamigbayan is a software engineer and business manager with over 8 years of experience. He holds a first-class degree in Computer Science from Bowen University and has completed courses in various fields such as IT Infrastructure Library, Lean Six Sigma, Business Analysis, and Project Management. He has managed multiple projects as a software engineer and process improvement consultant. Daniel also had a shot at management during his career at Stanbic IBTC bank where he emerged as a graduate trainee role model.

As a tech enthusiast and social entrepreneur, Daniel has taken several courses with the African Management Initiative and is an alumni of both the Bank of Industry Youth Entrepreneurship Support Programme and the Tony Elumelu Entrepreneurship Programme. He is also an Alumni of the Lagos Business School's Enterprise Development Centre. Daniel was voted among the top 10 IT gurus at Stanbic IBTC bank in Nigeria in 2014.

Daniel's technical expertise has enabled him to be part of several groundbreaking projects. He was on the team that developed the Nigerian Insurance Industry database and also on the team that developed Trago, an ERP and logistics management suite used to manage logistics for the Presidential Fertilizer Initiative. He is also the brain behind Pract+, a real-time proactivity productivity management system.

Apart from his technical expertise, Daniel is also passionate about making a positive impact through innovation. He is currently the CEO of D-Pia Innovations, a social enterprise that seeks to drive inclusive ICT skill acquisition and solve social and business problems with technology through collaboration. Lastly, Daniel's faith is an essential part of his life, and he is passionate about influencing young people to live a life of excellence and godliness despite the corrupting influence of society.
