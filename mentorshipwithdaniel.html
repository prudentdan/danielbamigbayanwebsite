Mentorship with Daniel B is an initiative of Daniel Bamigbayan. The goal of this initiative is to make myself available to guide young people who are on the path of discovery and wholesome living. It involves series of mentor learning sessions and group assignments.

By the end of the session, every participant would have been able to clarify their life mission and the small steps that must be taken to fulfil their long term goals . They would also be guided on how to access certain career path that aligns with their mission.

Every applicant will go through a selection process. Don't worry, if you dont make the first cohort, you can try next time I make a call.
